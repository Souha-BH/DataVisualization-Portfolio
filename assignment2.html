Visualization Makeover

Source
Before
After

The initial issue with the visualization was that it compared the unique visitor numbers of four news outlets using separate charts, each with different Y-axis values. This inconsistency was misleading and made it difficult to compare the performance across the four outlets accurately. Furthermore, displaying the charts separately gave the false impression that all four news outlets experienced similar percentage declines, while in reality, the Washington Post experienced a much more significant drop. To address these issues, I decided to plot all four trends on a single chart. By keeping the Y-axis consistent and starting from zero, we can ensure a clear and accurate comparison. Overall, all four news outlets experienced a notable decline in unique monthly visitors over the four-year period. The declining numbers suggest a broader trend of changing news consumption habits, where audiences might be turning to alternative digital platforms, social media, or niche news providers.
